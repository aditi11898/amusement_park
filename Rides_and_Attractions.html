Amusement Park

Amusement Park

12:30 PM – 09:00 PM
The Amusement Park has more than 20 fantastically built and placed rides and attractions to ensure that you have the time of your life when you visit with friends and family.
CHILL AND THRILL AMUSEMENT PARK is a world-class Amusement Park that doesn’t disappoint.
It is clean, green, and modern with a wide variety of attractions.
There are more than 20 rides that divided into two entertainment zones:
1) Khatron ke khel (High Thril Rides)
Road show meant for adventure lovers, has ten thrilling rides (also known as the teen zone).
2) La Fiesta(Kids and Family Rides)
La Fiesta meant for family and kids, comprises of 10 rides and a family park where the kids who are below 90 cm can enjoy their day. You can sit on the grass and have a small picnic or look at your little ones roll in laughter as they go about having fun on the non-mechanical rides and the older ones have a good time at other rides. It’s a family package of hearty moments and memories.

Water Park

Water Park

11:00 AM – 09:00 PM
If you are sick of the heat and daily hassle, CHILL AND THRILL WATER PARK is the perfect getaway to cool off and chill starting.
Bring your family to enjoy the thrill, the entertainment and the fun.
CHILL AND THRILL WATER PARK is considered the cleanest and well maintained Water Parks in the country and known for its kid friendly rides spread across 10 acres.
Each of our slides provide you with a different way of going with the flow.
Have a blast as you twist, turn, splash or just float around all day.
Take a pick from slides for the whole family and friends, indulge on some healthy competition.
Rest assured, whichever ride you choose, you’ll end up going right with the flow.
